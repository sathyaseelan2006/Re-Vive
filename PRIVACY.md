Privacy Policy (Draft)

This privacy policy describes how Re:Vive collects and uses data.

1. Data Collected
- Usage telemetry (events, timestamps, session duration)
- Anonymous aggregate analytics
- Optional user profile (if user signs up): username/email stored in MongoDB
- Audio generated for TTS (server) may be cached temporarily for performance

2. Use of Data
- Improve content recommendations and personalization
- Debug and monitor service health
- Do NOT sell personal data to third parties

3. Retention
- Telemetry retained for 12 months by default; user data retained until account deletion

4. Opt-out
- Users can opt-out from anonymous telemetry in settings or via cookie controls

5. Third-party services
- We use third-party APIs (Generative Model APIs, TTS providers), and these services may process data according to their policies. We do not share raw personally identifiable data with these providers unless required for service functionality.

6. Contact
- For privacy requests, contact the site administrator.
